Our Vision

Reimagining Education with AI

AI Teacher bridges the gap between passive video lecturing and interactive private tutoring, making premium personal education accessible worldwide.

The Mission

Traditional e-learning yields low completion rates because reading documents or watching static videos lacks dialogue and positive reinforcement. AI Teacher addresses this by humanizing learning.

By utilizing advanced vector avatars, custom binary PDF parses, and Google's multimodal Gemini architecture, we construct a responsive classroom where students sit across from a supportive virtual teacher.

Core Philosophy

  • 100% Privacy Secure All documents and local speech syntheses run directly inside the sandbox of your browser.
  • Conversational Reinforcement Elena bobs her head, nods, smiles, and uses verbal cues to praise students upon completing quizzes.
